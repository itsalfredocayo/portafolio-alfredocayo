**Plataforma:** LinkedIn
**Filtro Etnográfico:** Operacionalización de IA (Copilot, Prompts) en B2B, alineación táctica entre Ventas y Marketing, y la transición pragmática de SEO a GEO (Generative Engine Optimization).

### Ingeniería de Prompts B2B (Contexto y Especificidad)
* **Señal Técnica:** Framework estructurado para extraer valor de los LLMs (como ChatGPT) mediante la inyección de contexto de canales y análisis competitivo.
* **Contenido Crudo:**
> AI cannot think for itself. You are still the conductor of the AI symphony. There are three key practices to keep in mind when crafting prompts for LLMs: be specific and thorough, provide context, and keep trying!
> 
> Here's one of the B2B marketing prompts I’ve carefully crafted and tested:
> Marketing Channels: “My business wants to explore new marketing channels. [Describe what channels you currently use]. Historically, our best-performing channels have been [list channels], while our weakest have been [list]. [Give a demographic breakdown of your channel audiences and how they engaged]. Give [specific number] new marketing channels we should explore, supporting reasons why, and ideas on how we can implement them.”
> 
> Competitor Analysis: “Analyze of [name a top competitor] by using the SWOT analysis method. Then, list [specific number] ways a competitor to [name of top competitor again] could perform better in the [industry description].”

### Alineación Ventas-Marketing: Simplificación Despiadada
* **Señal Técnica:** Reducción de fricción cognitiva en la comunicación interna; cómo Marketing debe presentar a Ventas para asegurar adopción.
* **Contenido Crudo:**
> When presenting to sales, don't share everything - share what matters. Sales teams are busy. Their attention is short. I've learned that if you want your message to stick, you have to simplify it ruthlessly:
> 
> - Lead with one or two key takeaways — not ten.
> - Use visuals that are clean and scannable, not dense.
> - Share only what actually helps them sell.
> 
> Whether it's email sequencing or stage-based content, I always ask myself: am I building this for them, or for me? Present less. Land more.

### La Curva de Madurez IA en Empresas B2B
* **Señal Técnica:** Diagnóstico realista del estado actual de la adopción de IA corporativa: la dicotomía entre el uso individual (ejecución) y el uso de equipo (experimentación bloqueada por datos).
* **Contenido Crudo:**
> Where do most companies honestly sit on the AI maturity curve right now? Exploring, experimenting, or truly executing? Most companies are doing all three in parallel.
> 
> At the individual level: People are exploring and executing simultaneously. They're using chat-based tools to support their day-to-day work, while also testing other tools on the side.
> 
> At the team level: It's more about exploring and experimenting. Full execution gets complicated quickly—you need clean data, access to legacy systems, the ability for LLMs to pull the right information, and often some level of model training. There are a lot more moving parts to manage.

### Transición Pragmática de SEO a GEO (AI Search)
* **Señal Técnica:** Framework de decisión de 3 niveles para la adopción de "Generative Engine Optimization" basado en presupuesto, desmitificando la urgencia de abandonar el SEO tradicional.
* **Contenido Crudo:**
> How your customers are searching for information is changing - fast, but does that mean B2B marketers should ditch SEO and go all-in on GEO (Generative Engine Optimization)? Not so fast!
> 
> If you are navigating the shift to AI-driven search, here are three practical options to consider:
> 1. Do nothing (yet): SEO is not going away anytime soon. It is still a long game.
> 2. Do something: Keep your SEO efforts, but start amplifying your content and messaging across multiple channels to build broader authority.
> 3. Go all in: Restructure your content and site architecture specifically for AI-first search.
> 
> My take? There's no single "right" path—it's entirely budget-driven and depends on your resources. Start small. Optimize your high-value pages and focus on absolute clarity. Both AI models and human readers need to understand your value proposition.
