### Datos Duros: El impacto de la Automatización de Demos
* **Señal Técnica:** Métricas concretas sobre cómo la modularidad y la personalización de demos interactivas aceleran el cierre de ventas B2B.
* **Contenido Crudo:**
> After releasing our yearly State of the Interactive Demo report, we got some common feedback from customers. What if we use demos in my sales cycle instead of on the website? 
> 
> So this year, we created an entirely separate report - all around best practices and benchmarks for Demo Automation. Our first-ever State of Demo Automation Report analyzes: 40k+ demos built on Navattic, Survey data from 70+ SEs, Buyer engagement demo data.
> 
> A few stats that stood out to me:
> • 84% of automated demos are modular (prospects choose what they want to see upfront)
> • Personalized demos have nearly double the view rate (29% vs. 15%)
> • Deals with multiple automated demo touches close 19 days faster
> 
> The biggest takeaway for me: Automation works best when it’s personalized and used multiple times throughout a deal, not just sent once as a generic follow-up.
