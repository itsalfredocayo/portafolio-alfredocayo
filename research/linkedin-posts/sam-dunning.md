If I was a SaaS CMO needing demos in 90 days from SEO/AEO…I’d forget vanity traffic and prompt tracking and triple down on these 7 plays:

1. Nail your blueprint.

Map out every way that a prospect could search for your offering, compare vendors/alternatives, look for use cases/integrations etc. when they are in buying mode for your solution. 

2. Update and build an epic moat (website).

Use a tool like ahrefs to see which bottom funnel commercial pages you have already ranking but not #1 on Google or being picked up in AI search. Prio these gems for updates, linkbuilding and brand mentions for quick inbound. ChatGPT pulls from google.

Craft net new pages for bottom funnel pages you lack. Look what type of page (article, listicle, landing page) ranks on google or gets cited on AI search and blow it out the water. Fuel your page copy with customer research, unique stats product visuals, proof points, or reporting and address tough questions from sales calls. Ensure each page goes into detail and has a quick summary on the topic upfront with why you (LLMs can pull from this).

3. Leverage these pages cooking demos.

Build your own listicle articles for each competitor alternative term, best X software term, etc. craft yours to blow current pages out the water. Positions your software too backed by customer research, where you are a good and poor fit and a fair comparison of other vendors. Hugely important pages to control the narrative in your niche.

Find external “Top X software” and similar articles ranking well. Reach out to the authors and offer them a sweetener to include your brand.

You could offer to have them on your own listicle, offer a podcast slot, service exchange, social shoutout, etc. Some are pay to play. Many are up for value trade.

4. Win from past media.

Find an exec from your team that’s done a lot of press, podcasts etc. Put their Linkedin profile url into an SEO tool. Reach out to all relevant sites citing them that did not give you a brand mention or backlink to amend article. This is a huge quick win.

5. Control the narrative.

Google & LLMs love to cite Reddit. Start being helpful on relevant threads. Only mention your brand where it’s relevant. Start guesting on the top podcasts in your industry. Focus on a useful show for your ICP. Ask hosts to mention your brand and a backlink in the published article. If you can do all this and have resources for your own YouTube channel and podcast great, if not, prioritize the above tasks.

6. Speed beats huge players.

Do the above with speed and you’ll chip away at giant slow-moving competitors. They have slow approval processes with layers of management, legal etc. Speed will be your unfair advantage.

The pages they were building didn't have the right structure or hit-the-page intent to actually rank in organic and be structured well to be picked up on AI Search.

and demo driving pages that they did have weren't being regularly refreshed (e.g. every three months)

We recommend updating your best-converting bottom funnel pages first. AI Search loves fresh content. It's a simple way to get pages lagging bottom of page 1 on Google to the top and start to get cited in AI Search. 

A few ideas: 

- Make sure your page hits search intent 
- Pull insights from your sales & CS team
- Blow top ranking pages out the water
- Start with a quick summary upfront 
- Use tables if comparing options 
- Add unique data & research 
- Add relevant youtube vids 
- Add sales call FAQS
- Fix internal linking 

These updates are often the fastest way to up organic pipeline as quick as a few days after updates.
